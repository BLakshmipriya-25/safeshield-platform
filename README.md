# SafeShield – Women Safety & Cyber Protection Platform

SafeShield is a web-based platform designed to help women facing online harassment, blackmail, cyber threats, and digital abuse. 

Many victims hesitate to approach law enforcement due to fear, social pressure, or concerns about reputation. SafeShield provides a secure and confidential environment where victims can report incidents, upload evidence, and receive both legal and emotional support.

The platform creates a digital bridge between victims, police authorities, counselors, and administrators to ensure faster response, better support, and safer reporting.

---

## Problem Statement

Online harassment and cyber blackmail are increasing rapidly in the digital era. Victims often suffer silently because they are afraid to report incidents publicly.

SafeShield aims to solve this problem by providing:

- A secure complaint reporting platform
- Emotional support through counselors
- Efficient complaint management for police
- Administrative monitoring to prevent misuse

---

## System Architecture

SafeShield consists of four major modules:

1. Victim Portal  
2. Police Portal  
3. Counselor Portal  
4. Admin Portal  

Each module plays an important role in ensuring safety, privacy, and effective case management.

---

## Features

### Victim Portal

The Victim Portal allows women to safely report harassment or cybercrime incidents.

Victims can:

- Submit complaints securely
- Upload digital evidence such as screenshots or files
- Provide details about the harassment incident
- Share location information for investigation support
- Communicate directly with a counselor for emotional support

All victim data is handled securely to maintain privacy and confidentiality.

---

### Police Portal

The Police Portal allows authorized officers to manage and investigate complaints.

Police officers can:

- View registered complaints
- Access evidence uploaded by victims
- Review complaint details
- Update case status

Case statuses include:

- Under Review
- Investigation
- Closed

This helps authorities efficiently track and manage cyber harassment cases.

---

### Counselor Portal

Many victims of harassment experience emotional stress, anxiety, or trauma. 

The Counselor Portal allows trained counselors to support victims during difficult situations.

Counselors can:

- View assigned victim cases
- Communicate with victims through chat
- Provide psychological and emotional guidance
- Encourage victims and help them manage stress or fear

This ensures victims receive both legal assistance and emotional support.

---

### Admin Portal

The Admin Portal is responsible for managing and monitoring the entire system.

Admin responsibilities include:

- Monitoring complaints
- Managing users and system activity
- Detecting suspicious or fake complaints
- Ensuring the platform is used appropriately

The admin helps maintain system integrity and prevents misuse of the platform.

---

## Advanced Safety Features

### Fake Complaint Detection

To prevent misuse of the platform, SafeShield includes a fake complaint detection system.

This system analyzes complaints using:

- IP address tracking
- Duplicate complaint detection
- Message length and pattern analysis
- Suspicious activity monitoring

These checks help identify potentially false or repeated complaints for admin review.

---

### Red Alert Message Detection

SafeShield includes an alert detection mechanism to identify serious emotional distress.

If a victim sends messages that indicate:

- Severe emotional stress
- Panic
- Potential self-harm thoughts

The system automatically highlights the chat with a **Red Alert notification**.

This allows counselors to immediately prioritize and respond to critical situations.
